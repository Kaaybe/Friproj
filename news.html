Tenders, Bidders, Auctioneers, and Sales

Tenders Bidders Auctioneers Sales

Tenders

Tenders are formal invitations for suppliers to bid on contracts to supply goods or services. They are usually used for large-scale projects or purchases and are a way to ensure transparency and competitiveness in the procurement process.

Bidders

Bidders are individuals or organizations that submit proposals in response to a tender. They compete to offer the best terms, which can include price, delivery schedules, and quality of goods or services. The selection process is typically based on evaluating these proposals against predefined criteria.

Auctioneers

Auctioneers facilitate the auction process, where goods or services are sold to the highest bidder. They play a crucial role in managing the auction, setting the pace, and ensuring that the process runs smoothly. Auctioneers can be involved in various types of auctions, including live, online, and silent auctions.

Sales

Sales refer to the transaction process where goods or services are sold to buyers. This can include various methods such as direct sales, online sales, and auction sales. The sales process typically involves negotiation, closing the deal, and handling post-sale services.
